UMD Alive

UMD Alive is a group project I worked on for my Software Engineering class. UMD Alive meant for the use on the University of Minnesota-Duluth campus, and is designed specifically in-mind to work on bringing club information to the forefront, and make it easy to join, create, and find clubs. It is a platform for all of the on-campus clubs and students so that everyone can find a fun group with similar interests to join. It was created using Android Studio, and utilized Firebase and MongoDB.
